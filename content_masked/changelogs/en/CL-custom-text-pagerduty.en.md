The out-of-the-box [PagerDuty integration]([LINK_URL_1]) allows users to send their [Uptrends alerts]([LINK_URL_2]) directly to PagerDuty. As part of this update, the message we send to PagerDuty will now include the [custom message configured in the escalation levels]([LINK_URL_3]) of your alert definitions. You can find the message under **Message** in the **Custom details** of the PagerDuty alert.
